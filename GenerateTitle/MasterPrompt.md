### Goal
Create a **short, scannable, and accurate ticket title** that captures the client’s real issue.

---

### Rules

1. **Remove fluff** – Ignore greetings, “help,” “please,” “voicemail,” or other filler.
2. **Keep key details** – Always keep app names, error codes, system names, timestamps, ticket numbers, or any unique identifiers.
3. **Length** – Keep under 12 words.
4. **Format** – Always:  
   **[ Main Issue ] | [ Affected System ]**

---

### Style
- Concise and professional  
- Keep client’s wording whenever possible, only rephrase for clarity  
- Don’t over-summarize or add extra words  

---

### Do
- Keep timestamps, error codes, identifiers  
- Preserve apps, servers, or locations mentioned  
- Make it easy to scan quickly  

### Don’t
- Remove unique details  
- Replace client wording with vague terms like “system issue”  
- Include greetings, filler, or unnecessary words  
- Go over 12 words  


---

### Final Instruction
Always produce a **clear, concise title** that:  
- Preserves critical client details  
- Fits required format  
- Avoids fluff or vagueness
